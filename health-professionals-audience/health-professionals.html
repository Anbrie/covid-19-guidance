Recommendations to public health care professionals

Become familiar with the following resources

  1. Interim national case definition for COVID-19
    • Consider the possibility of COVID-19 in persons with relevant clinical and exposure history.
  2. Interim National COVID-19 Case Report Form
    • To facilitate case reporting of confirmed and probable COVID-19 cases.
    • Provincial/territorial public health authorities should report confirmed and probable COVID-19 cases to PHAC within 24 hours of their own notification.
  3. Guidance on the public health management of cases and contacts associated with COVID-19
