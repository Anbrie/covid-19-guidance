Use ArriveCan to enter Canada

We all have a part to play in keeping Canadians safe. Use the ArriveCAN app to slow the spread of COVID-19 at the border. Submit your information easily and securely within 48 hours before arriving in Canada.

Available for iOS, Android and as an accessible web form you fill out online.

The mobile app works in airplane mode on your smartphone. Use the mobile app or the web app to save your information to submit upon arrival at the airport or border or up to 48 hours ahead of time.

Provide your contact details and plan

To clear customs, use the ArriveCAN mobile app or the web app to provide:

  • contact information for you and for your family if you are travelling together
  • your 14-day isolation or quarantine plan for after you arrive in Canada

After you cross the border, use ArriveCAN to update your plan or contact details if they change.

You can also voluntarily provide updates about symptoms and confirm you are sticking with your quarantine plan.

Providing false or misleading information is an offence under the Quarantine Act and can result in fines and potentially prison time.

Your privacy is protected

The mobile ArriveCAN app and online web app:

  • do not use GPS or other technology to track your location
  • collect only the information needed to confirm you are complying the with the requirements to isolate or quarantine

Information may be disclosed to law enforcement agencies and provincial, territorial or munical institutions to administer and enforce the Quarantine Act and the Emergency Orders made under it.

For more, see the ArriveCAN Privacy notice
