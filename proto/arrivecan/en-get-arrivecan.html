How to use ArriveCAN

Use ArriveCAN to submit your information easily and securely before your arrival in Canada. ArriveCAN is available as a mobile app or you can sign in online.

Whether you’re entering Canada by air, land or sea, use ArriveCAN to:

  • provide required contact information for you and your family or group travelling together
  • answer questions about your quarantine plan if your trip is not exempt from quarantine
  • submit the travel information when it's complete
  • get a confirmation number to present to airline and border officials

After you arrive in Canada, use ArriveCAN to:

  • confirm that you've arrived at the address you provided for your quarantine location
  • complete COVID-19 symptom self-assessments during your 14-day mandatory quarantine or isolation period

For travellers without access to ArriveCAN

For travellers who don’t sign into ArriveCAN online or use the app to provide their travel information, a representative of the Government of Canada will call you. You’ll be contacted during your 14-day quarantine period after you arrive in Canada, so we can monitor compliance with your mandatory quarantine or isolation.

  • Make sure to answer calls from 1-888-336-7735

Travellers can also call a toll-free 1-833 number daily during this 14-day period to report on the possible development of COVID-19 symptoms.

Using ArriveCAN for travel exempt from quarantine

Certain travellers are exempt from the mandatory quarantine requirement under the Quarantine Act, including those entering Canada to perform:

  • medical support
  • essential services
  • trade or transport
  • cross-border work

If you aren't sure your travel is exempt from quarantine or which category it falls into Find out if your travel is exempt

While these exempt travellers aren’t required to quarantine upon entry, they’re still subject to other requirements. For instance, they must still provide accurate contact information when requested at the border.

Exempt travellers are encouraged to use ArriveCAN to keep and reuse your contact information for later travel. This saves you time and allows for faster processing at the border.

Your privacy is protected

ArriveCAN:

  • does not use GPS or other technology on your mobile phone to track your location
  • collects only the information needed to confirm you are complying the with the requirements to isolate or quarantine

The personal information provided through ArriveCAN is protected according to the Privacy Act. For more, see the ArriveCAN Privacy notice
