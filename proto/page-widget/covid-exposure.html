For travellers from outside of Canada

Federal and provincial public health authorities have recommended that all travellers from outside of Canada self-isolate for 14 days. These efforts will contribute to slowing the spread of COVID-19 in Canada.

If you develop a cough, fever or difficulty breathing within 14 days of travel:

  • Continue to isolate yourself from others
  • Immediately call a health care professional or public health authority and:
    • describe your symptoms and travel history
    • follow their instructions carefully

The following information is based on reports received from provincial, territorial and international health authorities, and media reports in some cases. This information is updated regularly but may be incomplete.
