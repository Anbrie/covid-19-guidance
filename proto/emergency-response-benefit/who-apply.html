Who can apply

If you can't work because of COVID-19 and you can't get Employment Insurance regular benefits or Employment Insurance sickness benefits, you can apply for the Canada Emergency Response Benefit.

To qualify, you must meet all of the following conditions:

    • Employment income
    • Self-employment income
    • Provincial benefit payments related to maternity or parental leave
    • Federal benefit payments related to maternity or parental leave
