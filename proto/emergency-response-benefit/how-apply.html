When to apply for the benefit

Applications for the Canada Emergency Response Benefit will begin the week of Monday, April 6, 2020.

The CRA cannot accept applications before then.

We expect a high number of applications

Whether you apply online or by phone, we want to provide the best service possible to everyone. To help manage this, we have set up specific days when you may apply. Please use the following guidelines:

  • You can apply on Mondays if you were born January to June
  • You can apply on Tuesdays if you were born July to December
  • You can apply on Wednesday to Sunday regardless of when you were born

How to apply for the benefit

You can apply for the Canada Emergency Response Benefit in 2 ways:

Online:
Apply online using your CRA My Account
By phone:
Call the automated toll-free line at 1-800-959-2019

Both of these services are available 21 hours a day, 7 days a week. Both services are closed from 3:00 a.m. to 6:00 a.m. (Eastern time) for maintenance.

What you can do now to get ready to apply

You will need access to My Account or My Service Canada Account (MSCA) to apply online for the Canada Emergency Response Benefit.

  1. If you do not currently have an account, register now
    • My Account
    • My Service Canada Account (you can use your MSCA account to access the CRA’s My Account)
  2. Make sure your direct deposit and mailing information is up to date with the CRA. You can review and update both online in My Account

Note: The following financial institutions can also securely update your direct deposit information with the CRA.

  • Desjardins members can sign up online
  • TD Canada Trust customers can sign up by phone or in person at their branch

How to apply online

To apply for the Canada Emergency Response Benefit:

  1. Log in to My Account and click on the link in the COVID-19: Canada Emergency Response Benefit alert banner at the top of the page
  2. Select the period you want to apply for
  3. Declare that you qualify for the benefit
  4. Confirm we have the right payment information

You will need to apply and confirm your eligibility for the Canada Emergency Response Benefit every 4 weeks if your situation continues.

How to apply by phone

Make sure you have the following information with you when you apply by phone:

  • your social insurance number (SIN)
  • mailing address

When you call the toll-free number, you will be connected to the CRA's Interactive Voice Response (IVR) system. It will prompt you to enter the necessary information by pressing the telephone keypad numbers.

To apply by phone:

  1. Call 1-800-959-2019
  2. Select your language preference: English or French
  3. Follow the prompts to enter your information, including:
    • your SIN
    • confirmation of your mailing address
    • the period you are applying for
  4. Declare that you qualify for the benefit

The CRA will issue payments by direct deposit or cheque, based on the payment method you have on file.

You will need to apply and confirm your eligibility for the Canada Emergency Response Benefit every 4 weeks if your situation continues.
