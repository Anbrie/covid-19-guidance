How to apply

Answer a few questions to determine whether to apply for the Canada Emergency Response Benefit with EI or through a CRA My Account.

Thinking about your main source of employment income in the last year - were you an employee or self-employed?

  • I was self-employed
  • I was an employee

Were you a full-time employee for at least four months last year? (“full-time” means someone who works 30 or more hours per week)

  • Yes
  • No

Were you a part-time employee for at least eight months last year? (part-time means someone who works less than 30 hours per week)

  • Yes
  • No
