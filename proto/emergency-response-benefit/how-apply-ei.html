Apply through EI

To be eligible to receive the CERB from Service Canada, the following must apply:

  • You are a resident of Canada
  • You are 15 years of age or older at the time of application
  • If you are submitting your application for the first time, that you have stopped or will stop working for reasons related to COVID-19 for at least 14 consecutive days [within the 4 week benefit period]
  • If you are filing for a subsequent benefit period, that you do not expect to have any employment or self-employment income
  • You have not quit your job voluntarily
  • You are not receiving nor have you applied for the CERB from the Canada Revenue Agency for the same entitlement period
  • You have earned a minimum of $5,000 income within the last 12 months or in the 2019 tax year from one or more of the following sources:
    • Employment income
    • Self-employment income
    • Provincial benefit payments related to maternity or parental leave
    • Federal benefit payments related to maternity or parental leave

Eligibility periods:

  • The maximum entitlement is a total of 16 weeks
  • Eligibility periods are fixed in four week periods

Note: If you are later deemed ineligible, you will be expected to repay the benefit
