Get ready to apply before April 6

Applications for the CERB will begin the week of Monday, April 6, 2020.

Although you can't apply yet, you should get ready by making sure you have a CRA account and updated profile:

Do you have a CRA My Account?

  • Yes - I have a CRA My Account

    Make sure your direct deposit and mailing information is up to date with the CRA.

    Applications open on April 6.

    Sign in to your CRA account

  • Yes - but I'm locked out

    To recover your CRA My Account and apply for the Canada Emergency Response Benefit, you will have to call and get a new security code.

    Telephone number: 1-800-387-1193

  • No - but I have a My Service Canada Account for EI

    Sign in to CRA My Account with a My Service Canada Account

    Since you have a My Service Canada Account, you do not need to register for a CRA My Account.

    After you sign in to My Service Canada Account:

    1. Look for the link to "Switch to Canada Revenue Agency" and then "agree" on the Registration and authentication page
    2. Update your address and direct deposit with CRA (CRA does not have your personal information from your My Service Canada Account)
    3. You can then complete the application when it becomes available on April 6th.

    Applications open on April 6.

    Sign in to My Service Canada Account

  • No - I don't have either

    Register for a CRA My Account to apply for the Canada Emergency Response Benefit

    Register for CRA My Account

Is your account information up-to-date?

  • Yes – My direct deposit and mailing information is up to date with the CRA

    Great. If you have a CRA My Account and your deposit and mailing information is up to date, you are ready to apply for CERB after April 6.

  • No - I need to update direct deposit and mailing information

    Sign in to your My CRA Account to validate that we have your proper information.

    The following financial institutions can also securely update your direct deposit information with the CRA.

    • Desjardins members can sign up online
    • TD Canada Trust customers can sign up by phone or in person at their branch

    Sign in to your CRA My Account

Select the best day of the week to apply after April 6

Whether you apply online or by phone, we want to provide the best service possible to everyone. To help manage this, we have set up specific days when you may apply. Please use the following guidelines:

Day to apply for the Canada Emergency Response Benefit
If you were born in the month of You can apply for CERB on
January, February or March Mondays
April, May, or June Tuesdays
July, August, or September Wednesdays
October, November, or December Thursdays
Everyone Fridays, Saturdays and Sundays

Apply again every 4 weeks

You will need to apply and confirm your eligibility for the Canada Emergency Response Benefit every 4 weeks if your situation continues.
