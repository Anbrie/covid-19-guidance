Applications open on April 6th

Applications for the Canada Emergency Response Benefit will begin the week of Monday, April 6, 2020.

The CRA cannot accept applications before then.

Make sure you have an account and updated profile

Although you can't yet apply yet, you should get ready by making sure you have a CRA account:

Get ready to apply

How to apply

You must apply on a specific day

Whether you apply online or by phone, we want to provide the best service possible to everyone. To help manage this, we have set up specific days when you may apply. Please use the following guidelines:

  • You can apply on Mondays if you were born January to June
  • You can apply on Tuesdays if you were born July to December
  • You can apply on Wednesday to Sunday regardless of when you were born
Online:

Sign into your CRA account

Instructions once you're in your CRA account
  1. Go to COVID-19: Canada Emergency Response Benefit in the alert banner at the top of the page
  2. Select the period you want to apply for
  3. Declare that you qualify for the benefit
  4. Confirm we have the right payment information
By phone:
Call the automated toll-free line
Before you call

To verify your identity, you'll need

  • your social insurance number (SIN)
  • mailing address

If you are calling the CRA on behalf of someone else, you must be an authorized representative

Telephone number

1-800-959-2019

Instructions
  1. Select your language preference: English or French
  2. Follow the prompts to enter your information, including:
    • your SIN
    • confirmation of your mailing address
    • the period you are applying for
  3. Declare that you qualify for the benefit

The CRA will issue payments by direct deposit or cheque, based on the payment method you have on file.

Hours
Always open (automated line)

Apply again every 4 weeks

You will need to apply and confirm your eligibility for the Canada Emergency Response Benefit every 4 weeks if your situation continues.
