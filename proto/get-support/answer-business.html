Find out what support you can apply for during the COVID-19 outbreak: Support for your business

Your business should:

  • Apply for funding programs at Farm Credit Canada - funding opportunities for farmers
  • Apply for for funding through Business Credit Availability Program - for businesses that need financing
  • Apply for Emergency Response Benefits - for self-employed individuals who won't be able to work and don't contribute to EI plans
  • Check if you qualify for 75% reduced payroll payments
  • Apply for work sharing benefits - to support employees who agree to reduce their normal working hours
  • Tell employees they can apply for benefits - if they have reduced hours or are layed off during COVID-19
  • Get emergency loans for Canadians abroad to return to Canada
  • Get support from trade commissioner services - for business support abroad
  • Defer your tax payments until August 31, 2020
  • Take advantage of lower interest rate and domestic stability buffer
