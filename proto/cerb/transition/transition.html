Step 1: Find out if you are eligible for EI

Employment Insurance eligibility has changed. Before you apply for other Recovery Benefits, find out if you may be eligible for the $400 minimum weekly regular benefit payments or $240 minimum weekly extended parental benefits:

You should apply for EI benefits so EI processing agents can confirm that you are eligible.

What to expect when you apply

There are some differences between CERB and EI you should understand before you apply:

  • Access to the EI online application is not through an account. No login is required.
  • Employment Insurance does not have fixed eligibility periods.
  • You apply once for EI and then report every two weeks on your efforts to find work.
  • View your claim status in My Service Canada account by signing into your CRA My Account.
  • EI regular benefits start at $400 per week and may be higher based on your best weeks of insurable income.
