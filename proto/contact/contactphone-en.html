Answers to top questions

If you already applied for Employment Insurance (EI)

If you’ve already applied for EI, you will get CERB automatically. You don’t need to apply again, but you must do your Internet report every 2 weeks.

If you received two payments of $2000

If you applied for EI and then applied for CERB, you might have gotten two $2000 payments. Don’t worry, we will contact you shortly.

Do I need to reapply for CERB?

Eligibility periods are fixed in 4-week periods. If your situation continues, you can re-apply for CERB for an additional 4-week period, to a maximum of 16 weeks (4 periods). See the specific dates here:

ask CRA for anchor to dates
I just applied for CERB, when should I expect to get the money?

Benefits will start within 10 days after you submit a CERB application, there’s no waiting period. Payments will be made through direct deposit or by cheque. You’ll be paid more quickly if you choose direct deposit.
