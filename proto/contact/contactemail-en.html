Canadian Emergency Response Benefit (CERB)

If you already applied for Employment Insurance (EI)

If you’ve already applied for EI, you will get CERB automatically. You don’t need to apply again.

If you received two payments of $2000

If you applied for EI and then applied for CERB, you might have gotten two $2000 payments. Don’t worry, we will contact you shortly to discuss.

Do I have to apply for the next Canada Emergency Response Benefit period or is it automatic? When can I reapply for the next period?

Eligibility periods are fixed in 4-week periods. If your situation continues, you can re-apply for CERB for an additional 4-week period, to a maximum of 16 weeks (4 periods). See the specific dates.

What if I applied through EI?

You do not have to reapply but you must do your Internet report every 2 week to continue recieving payments

I just finished applying for the Canada Emergency Response Benefit. When should I expect to get the money?

Benefits will start within 10 days after you submit a CERB application, there’s no waiting period. Payments will be made through direct deposit or by cheque. You’ll be paid more quickly if you choose direct deposit.
