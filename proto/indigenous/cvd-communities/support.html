Indigenous individuals

Indigenous peoples can apply for programs available from the Government of Canada.

If you have stopped working because of COVID-19, you can:

  • apply for the Canada Emergency Response Benefit (CERB)
    • if you don't have a CRA My Account, or have never filed a tax return, please call 1-800-959-8281 for help applying for the CERB

  • consult additional support for individuals

Indigenous students

Post-secondary students and recent graduates who cannot find work as a result of the COVID-19 pandemic can:

  • apply for the Canada Emergency Student Benefit (CESB)

To support students pursuing post-secondary education an additional $75.2 million will be provided. Information on how you can access this money will be available soon.

  • Post-secondary education

Indigenous organizations and communities

New services are being added to support organizations and communities during this difficult time.

To address immediate needs in First Nations, Inuit, and Métis Nation communities a new fund of $305 million was created:

  • Indigenous Community Support Fund

Indigenous businesses

Small and medium-sized Indigenous businesses can:

  • apply for an interest-free loan up to $30,000 and a non-repayable contribution up to $10,000
  • apply for the Canada Emergency Wage Subsidy (CEWS)
  • answer a few questions to get a personalized list of support
  • consult additonal support for businesses
