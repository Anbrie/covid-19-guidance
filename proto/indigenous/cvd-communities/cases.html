Confirmed cases of COVID-19

On First Nations reserves in provinces, as of May 149, Indigenous Services Canada (ISC) is aware of:

  • 198 confirmed positive COVID-19
  • 18 hospitalizations
  • 3 deaths

Case numbers per region:

  • British Columbia: 39
  • Alberta: 36
  • Saskatchewan: 46
  • Ontario: 42
  • Quebec: 35
