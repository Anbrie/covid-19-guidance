Confirmed cases of COVID-19

As of April 29, ISC is aware of 129 confirmed positive COVID-19 tests on First Nations reserves in provinces.

  • British Columbia: 33
  • Alberta: 20
  • Saskatchewan: 14
  • Ontario: 32
  • Quebec: 30

Provincial chief public health officers work with ISC's regional medical officers and nurses to provide medical support as needed when a positive case is reported.

Visit: Coronavirus disease (COVID-19): Current situation for the total number of confirmed or probable cases of COVID-19 across Canada.
