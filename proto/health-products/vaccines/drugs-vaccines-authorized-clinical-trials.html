The table below lists the COVID-19 drugs, including vaccines, currently being investigated in clinical trials authorized by Health Canada. Authorization is through the Food and Drug Regulations or the interim order for clinical trials for medical devices and drugs related to COVID-19.

Health Canada continues to discuss potential clinical trials with a number of sponsors. We will update the list as new trials related to drugs or vaccines for COVID-19 are authorized.
