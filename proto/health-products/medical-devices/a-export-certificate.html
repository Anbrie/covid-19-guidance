Requires an export certificate

Canadian companies who solely want to export medical devices can apply for Manufacturer's Certificate to Export (MCE). This certificate isn't a regulatory requirement.

This certificate proves that your medical devices meet Canadian regulations and can be marketed in Canada. It includes an attestation by both the manufacturer and by Health Canada.

Requirements you must meet to apply for MCE

Health Canada will only issue a manufacturer's certificate if the following requirements are met:

  • you are located in Canada
  • you hold one of the following licences:
    • active medical device licence (MDL) for Class II, III and IV devices
    • active medical device establishment licence (MDEL) for Class I devices
