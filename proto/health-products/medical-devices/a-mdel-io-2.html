Requires an MDEL and exceptional Interim Order

You require both an MDEL and exceptional Interim Order (different from the medical device IO)

Step 1: Apply for a Medical Device Establishment Licence (MDEL)

Your company needs an MDEL.

To submit an MDEL application

  1. Review Guidance 0016: Medical device establishment licensing and medical device establishment licensing fees.
  2. Complete the MDEL application form (FRM-0292)
  3. Email the completed MDEL application form to
  4. E-mail: hc.mdel.application.leim.sc@canada.ca
    Subject line: URGENT - COVID-19 - MDEL application for - [name of company]

AND

Step 2: Apply for authorization under the exceptional Interim Order

You must already hold an MDEL before you can apply for authorization under the exceptional Interim Order to import or distribute medical devices that do not meet all labelling standards.

There are 2 different Interim Orders in place during COVID-19 for medical devices.

You must apply for authorization under the Interim Order Respecting Drugs, Medical Devices and Foods for a Special Dietary Purpose in relation to COVID-19 (also known as Exceptional IO) to import and/or distribute medical devices that do not meet all labelling standards.

These are granted by the Regulatory Operations & Enforcement Branch.
