Option 1: Apply for authorization under the Interim Order

Expedited process during COVID-19

You must apply for authorization for each type of device you manufacture, and Interim Order authorizations will expire once the COVID-19 pandemic ends.

You should apply for authorization under the Interim Order, which allows expedited access to COVID-19-related medical devices. It applies to manufacturers of Class I to IV medical devices.

Under the IO, manufacturers will be required to submit an abbreviated application to support the safety, effectiveness and quality of their medical device. The fees associated with an application through the IO pathway will be waived. This is the most effective pathway manufacturers should be following in order to apply for authorization.

Apply for authorization under the Interim Order

OR

Option 2: Apply for a Medical Device Establishment Licence (MDEL)

The manufacturer can apply for an MDEL for their company. This option will continue to be valid after the COVID-19 pandemic.

To submit an MDEL application

  1. Review Guidance 0016: Medical device establishment licensing and medical device establishment licensing fees.
  2. Complete the MDEL application form (FRM-0292)
  3. Email the completed MDEL application form to
  4. E-mail: hc.mdel.application.leim.sc@canada.ca
    Subject line: URGENT - COVID-19 - MDEL application for - [name of company]

Not sure which to apply for?

Comparison between options
What is being compared IO Submission MDEL Submission
Who can apply? Class I, II, III and IV manufacturers can apply. Importers/distributors (of any class of device) and Class I manufacturers can apply.
Who signs the application? Application requires manufacturer’s signature. If the applicant is the importer/distributor, the application does not require manufacturer’s signature.
Who is the authorization issued to? The IO authorization is issued to the manufacturer. The MDEL is issued to the applicant (importer/distributor or Class I manufacturer).
What needs to be submitted? An IO Request Form and IO submission package awith all supporting documentation as outlined in the IO guidance. An MDEL application form.
Is device labelling required? Yes, must submit a copy of the device label. No device label required.
What are the fees? No fee. $4,590, but the MDEL holder can qualify for a fee remission.
