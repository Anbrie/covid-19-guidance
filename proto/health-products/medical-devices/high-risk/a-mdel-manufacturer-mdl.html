MDEL and manufacturer MDEL

You require an MDEL and the manufacturer requires a Medical Device Licence

Step 1: Apply for a Medical Device Establishment Licence

You can apply for an MDEL for your company for all medical devices that you import or distribute. This option will continue to be valid after the COVID-19 pandemic and can be used for importing or distributing other devices.

Apply for a Medical Device Establishment Licence (MDEL)

AND

Option 2: Manufacturer applies for an Medical Device Licence or authorization under the Interim Order for the medical device

The manufacturer can apply for a Medical Device Licence for the device. This option will continue to be valid after the COVID-19 pandemic.
