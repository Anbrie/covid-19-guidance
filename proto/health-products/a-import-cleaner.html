Interim policy to address shortages during COVID-19

The COVID-19 pandemic has created an unprecedented demand for household cleaning products regulated under the Canada Consumer Product Safety Act (CCPSA). As an interim policy due to the COVID-19 pandemic, we’re facilitating the access and sale of certain consumer products in Canada where:

  • the label may only be in one official language
  • the label may be different from what is required for sale in Canada
