Manufacture, import or distribute sanitizers in Canada

You must register with the Pest Management Regulatory Agency (PMRA)

You must register with the Pest Management Regulatory Agency (PMRA) to manufacture, import or distribute:

  • hard or soft surface sanitizers
  • self-sanitizing surfaces or coatings
  • water or air sanitizers

Before submitting an application

We recommend you to request a pre-submission consultion at no cost to:

  • confirm if the proposed product and technical grade active ingredients are eligible for review
  • understand the required information to support the health, environment, chemistry, and value evaluations
  • get guidance on building a successful submission package for the product
