Manufacturing, packaging, labelling, and/ or importing ethanol based hand sanitizer on behalf of another company

You require a site licence

You must receive authorization from Health Canada’s Natural and Non-prescription Health Products Directorate

A site licence is required to manufacture, package, label and/or import an NHP hand sanitizer in Canada. Please note that a site licence is required for the production of finished NHPs, and is not required for the production of raw materials.

Apply for a site licence
