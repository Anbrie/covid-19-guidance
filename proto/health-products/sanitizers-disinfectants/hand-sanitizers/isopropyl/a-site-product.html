Manufacture and distribute alcohol based hand sanitizer in Canada

You require both a site and product licence and must meet quality requirements

Step 1: Apply for both a site and product licence

A site licence is required to manufacture, package, label and/or import an NHP hand sanitizer in Canada. Please note that a site licence is required for the production of finished NHPs, and is not required for the production of raw materials.

A product licence, represented by a Natural Product Number (or NPN), is required to legally distribute (i.e., donate or sell) the product. A product licence is required even if donating these products.
