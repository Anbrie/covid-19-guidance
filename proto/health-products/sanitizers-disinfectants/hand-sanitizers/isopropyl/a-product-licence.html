Distribute isopropyl alcohol based hand sanitizer in Canada made in Canada by another company

You require a product licence

You must receive authorization from Health Canada’s Natural and Non-prescription Health Products Directorate

A product licence, represented by a Natural Product Number (or NPN), is required to legally distribute (i.e., donate or sell) the product. A product licence is required even if donating these products.
