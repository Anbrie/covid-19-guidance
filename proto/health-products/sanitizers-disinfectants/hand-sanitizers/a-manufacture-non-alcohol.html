Manufacture non-alcohol based hand sanitizers or antimicrobial hand soaps in Canada

You must receive authorization from Health Canada’s Natural and Non-prescription Health Products Directorate

To obtain an authorization to market a non-alcohol based hand sanitizer or an antimicrobial hand soap that meets the parameters in the monograph, please follow these steps:

Step 1: Ensure the sanitizer meets quality standards

You will need a certificate attesting that the quality of the product meets the standards in the

  • Antiseptic Skin Cleansers Monograph

AND

Step 2: Apply for a drug establishment licence

You must meet the Good Manufacturing Practices outlined in the Food and Drug Regulations

We ask that you certify the standards being used and while a Drug Establishment Licence application is normally required, Health Canada is exempting this requirement as an interim measure. However, products are still expected to maintain Good Manufacturing Practices requirements.

AND

Step 3: Complete a Drug Submission Application Form

You will need to submit a Drug Submission Application form and get a Drug Identification Number (DIN) before you can manufacture and distribute non-alcohol based sanitizers or antimicrobial hand soaps.

The following must be included in your application:

  • Cover letter
  • Canadian label materials (e.g. label mock-up, package inserts – as applicable)
  • If using the Regulatory Enrolment Process (REP)
    • Regulatory Transaction XML
    • Product Information XML (as required)
  • If not using the Regulatory Enrolment Process (REP)
    • Drug Submission Application Form (3011)
    • Drug Submission - Application Fee Form for Human and Disinfectant Drugs

The following might be required in your application depending on your circumstances:

  • Non-prescription drug monograph attestation form
  • Drug Identification Number (DIN) Submission Certification for Human and Disinfectant Drugs
  • Letter of Authorization And Administrative Changes - Certification Form for Human and/or Disinfectant Drug Submissions and Applications
  • Labels and Packages Certification Form for Non-prescription drugs

AND

Step 4: Submit your application package

Please see the Guidance Document: Management of Drug Submissions that outlines the submission process

For products outside of the monograph parameters, please consult Guidance Document - Human-Use Antiseptic Drugs
