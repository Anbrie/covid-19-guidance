Import hand sanitizers or disinfectants for distribution in Canada

You will need to notify Health Canada

As an interim measure due to the COVID-19 pandemic, we are making it possible for certain products to be sold in Canada. Products that are already approved by the United States Food and Drug Administration (FDA) or the United States Environmental Protection Agency (EPA), a Pharmaceutical Inspection Cooperation Scheme (PICS) country or a Mutual Recognition Agreement (MRA) country can be imported using a simplified process. These products may not fully meet regulatory requirements, but do not compromise the safety of Canadians.

Importers must:

  • notify us prior to importing or manufacturing and wait to obtain an authorization email from us
  • keep records in the event of a product recall
  • indicate on their notification form the official language of the product label (French, English or both) and, if applicable, the website where the bilingual product label can be found
  • meet certain labelling requirements
