Import, manufacture or distribute workplace cleaning products

You must follow all labelling and applicable safety data sheet requirements

All labelling and as applicable, safety data sheet requirements remain in place.

You must:

  • identify if your product is a hazardous product
  • prepare labels and safety data sheets (SDS) in both official languages
  • provide the labels and safety data sheets (SDS) to purchasers of your products
  • file a claim to be exempt from disclosing confidential business information, in accordance with Hazardous Materials Information Review Act (HMIRA).

To understand all requirements and regulations, consult:

  • Hazardous Products Act (HPA)
  • Hazardous Products Regulations (HPR)
  • Hazardous Materials Information Review Act (HMIRA)

If you have questions about importing, manufacturing or distributing workplace cleaners, you can contact us by sending an e-mail to hc.whmis-simdut.sc@canada.ca.
