Special circumstances

Medical gloves

If you manufacture medical gloves, you must clearly explain the intended use of a device if it is not clear. You can accomplish this using specific words and images. For example:

  • words such as "health care," "examination," "patient examination," "sterile," or "medical grade"
  • images or symbols that signify a medical use (e.g., a cross)

These labels are necessary because not all disposable gloves are manufactured or sold for use as medical gloves. Consumers also use disposable gloves in car repair shops or for personal use in their homes.
