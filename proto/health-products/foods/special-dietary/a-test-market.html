You may be eligible for Test market authorization

Companies may request an exemption to test market a product that is not in compliance with:

  • bilingual labelling requirements of the Food and Drug Regulations (FDR) and the Safe Food for Canadians Regulations (SFCR)
  • certain provisions in the SFCR (e.g. standard container size requirements)

A test market food is only exempt from certain regulatory provisions and must comply with applicable legislation in all other respects.

To be eligible, food product must meet all of the following conditions:

  • food product has not previously been sold in Canada in that form
  • food product differs substantially from any other food sold in Canada with respect to its composition, function, state or packaging form
