Exceptional importation and sale of foods for special dietary purpose

You may be eligible to import or sell this product through the Interim Order

Step 1: Submit a request to get the product "Designated"

You need to submit a request to Bureau of Nutritional Sciences to have the product added to the List of foods for special dietary purpose for exceptional importation and sale.

To sumbit a request, contact the Bureau of Nutritional Sciences, Food Directorate via phone at (613 948-8476) or via email at hc.bns-bsn.sc@canada.ca.

Step 2: Once added to the list, notify the Minister five calendar days before importation

If your request is approved, your product will become "designated" and added to the List of Foods for Special Dietary Purpose.

As an importer, you must:

  • for each time you import the product, notify the Minister at least five calendar days before the day of importation
    • Designated Food for Special Dietary Purpose Notification Form
  • ensure that product information is available on both English and French for safe preparation and use of the food, including:
    • special dietary purpose for which the food is represented
    • warnings (if applicable)
    • directions for the preparation, use and storage of the food
    • expiration date
    • the lot number of the food
    • the list of food ingredients, including priority allergens

For more information, consult:

  • Guidance document: exceptional importation and sale of food for special dietary purpose
