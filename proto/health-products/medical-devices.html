Medical devices

What medical device are you handling?

  • Ventilators, oxygen masks, breathing circuits/tubing, and accessories
  • Personal Protective Equipment (medical masks, medical gloves, medical gowns, etc)
  • Testing devices
  • Flock swabs or sample collection swabs
  • Thermometers
  • Isolation chambers
  • Infusion pumps, catheters, dialysis or oxygenation devices or extra-corporeal membranes
  • None of the above

Medical device accessories: intended to support, supplement, and/or augment the performance of one or more parent device may also require licensing. Consultation with Medical Devices Directorate may be required (email: hc.meddevices-instrumentsmed.sc@canada.ca)
