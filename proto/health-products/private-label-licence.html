You require a private label medical device licence

Based on your answers, the medical device must have a private label medical device licence.

The medical device licence is required for distribution (to sell) in Canada or for export for use in other countries if it is manufactured in Canada.

  • Apply for a private label medical device licence

Note: If you have a medical device licence, you do not need a medical device establishment licence (MDEL) to import or sell that medical device.
