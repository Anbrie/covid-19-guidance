Canada Emergency Wage Subsidy (CEWS)

As a Canadian employer who has seen a drop in business due to COVID-19, you may be eligible for a subsidy to cover part of your employee wages, retroactive to March 15. This subsidy will enable you to re-hire workers, help prevent further job losses, and ease you back into normal operations.

New Updates to CEWS were announced July 17. Changes beginning in period 5 (July 5) include:

  • as an eligible employer you no longer need to have lost at least 30% of your revenue to qualify
  • the amount you may receive depends on how much revenue you’ve lost
