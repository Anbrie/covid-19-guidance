Periods you can apply for

Base revenue drop comparison

General:

  • July 2020 over July 2019, or
  • June 2020 over June 2019 online calculator or spreadsheet
or

Alternative:

  • July 2020 over the average of January and February 2020, or
  • June 2019 over the average of January and February 2020
Base rate

If base revenue drop is 50% or more:

  • 60%
or

If base revenue drop is less than 50%:

  • 1.2 x (base revenue drop)
Top-up revenue drop comparison period

General:

  • Average of April, May, and June, 2020
or

Alternate:

  • Average of January and February, 2020
Top-up rate

If top-up revenue drop is 70% or more:

  • 25%
or

If top-up revenue drop is 50% to 69.9999%:

  • 1.25 x (average revenue drop - 50%)

Note: There is no top-up rate if top-up revenue drop is less than 50%

Overall CEWS rate

For active employees:

  • base rate + top-up rate
  • Minimum of 75%, if your revenue drop was at least 30% (read about the “safe harbour” option)
or

For eligible employees on leave with pay:

  • 75%
Employee pre-crisis remuneration period

January 1 to March 15, 2020

or

July 1, 2019, to December 31, 2019
