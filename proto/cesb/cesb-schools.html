Canada Emergency Student Benefit (CESB): Eligible post-secondary schools

To apply for the Canada Emergency Student Benefit (CESB) you must confirm that you are registered in a post-secondary program.

Find the name of the post-secondary school where you are registered

Many Canadian schools are listed in the Master List of designated educational institutions. This list is updated regularly and contains all the educational institutions where students are eligible to receive Canada Student Loans.

If you school isn't included in the Master List of instutions

Some private training institutions or educational programs in Canada aren't included in the Master List. Look for your program or school in the provincial and territorial lists of certified educational institutions and student aid services.
