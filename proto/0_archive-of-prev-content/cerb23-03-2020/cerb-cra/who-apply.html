Who can apply

You cannot apply for the CERB with CRA if you already applied through Service Canada

If you have already applied for Employment Insurance (EI) or for the CERB through Service Canada, you cannot apply for the CERB with the CRA.

If you are applying for the CERB for the first time, and not sure where to apply, answer the questions under Get started, on the Canada Emergency Response Benefit page. We will direct you to the service option that best fits your situation.

To be eligible, you must meet of the following conditions:

    What counts towards the $1,000 in income I can earn?

You are eligible and ready to proceed

The current eligibility period is April 12, 2020 to May 9, 2020. The next eligibility period is May 10, 2020 to June 6, 2020.

We verify eligibility

The CRA will verify that you are eligible to receive the CERB. If you apply but are not eligible, we will contact you to make arrangements to repay any amounts you may owe.
