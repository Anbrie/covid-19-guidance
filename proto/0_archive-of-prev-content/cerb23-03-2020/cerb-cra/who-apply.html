Who can apply

You cannot apply for the CERB with CRA if you already applied through Service Canada

If you are applying for the CERB for the first time and are not sure where to apply, answer the questions under Determine where to apply, on the Canada Emergency Response Benefit page. We will direct you to the service option that best fits your situation.

Verifying your eligibility

The CRA will be verifying that you are eligible to receive the benefit. In cases where claimants are found to be ineligible, they will be contacted to make arrangements to repay any applicable amounts. Canadians can also report suspected CERB misuse through the CRA’s Leads program.

As part of the Canada Emergency Wage Subsidy, the CRA will be comparing employers’ payroll records with information provided by CERB claimants to ensure that individuals who have returned to work and who have therefore become ineligible for the CERB repay those amounts.
