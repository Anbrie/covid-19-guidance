Canada Emergency Response Benefit (CERB)

What is the Canada Emergency Response Benefit

If you have lost your employment income because of COVID-19, the Canada Emergency Response Benefit (CERB) will provide you with temporary income support.

The CERB will provide you with $500 a week for up to 16 weeks. There is no waiting period and direct deposit payments will be made within three (3) days of submitting your application (10 days if by cheque).

We are targeting April 6, 2020 as the date when we can start accepting applications.

How to apply

Service Canada and the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) deliver this benefit jointly. You can apply through one or the other, but not both. If you have already applied for Employment insurance, you do not need to re-apply

Please answer the following questions to help you decide how to apply for the CERB. Your responses will not affect your eligibility for benefits and we will not keep this information. Please answer truthfully.
