  • a foreign national

You must meet the entry requirements under the Immigration and Refugee Protection Act and provide appropriate travel and immigration documentation. Final determination regarding entry is made by a government representative at the port of entry based on the information presented to them at the time of entry into Canada. Failure to comply with the COVID-19 Emergency order is an offence under the Quarantine Act and could lead to imprisonment and/or fines.

Based on your answers, you will likely be allowed in Canada.

Canadian Forces or visiting forces

Canadian Forces members and visiting forces are generally exempt from quarantine for situations described under essential work.

If a government representative at the border determines you are not quarantine exempt

You must:

  • follow a mandatory quarantine period of 14 days
  • have a suitable place to quarantine

You may have to comply with additional provincial or territorial requirements.

Their immediate family members

Who is considered immediate family

To be considered an immediate family member you must be the:

  • spouse or common-law partner of the person
  • dependent child of the person
  • dependent child of a dependent child of the person
  • parent or step-parent of the person
  • guardian or tutor of the person

Immediate family members must generally:

  • follow a mandatory quarantine period of 14 days
  • have a suitable place to quarantine

It's possible an immediate family member could be exempt from quarantine if they are entering for an essential reason.

You may have to comply with additional provincial or territorial requirements.
