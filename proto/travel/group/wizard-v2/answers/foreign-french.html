Who can come to Canada - Foreign national without symptoms - French

This tool is up to date with the Orders in Council published on 2020-06-29

You indicated that:

  • you are a foreign national
  • you are not showing signs and symptoms consistent with COVID-19
  • you are a French citizen who lives in Saint-Pierre and Miquelon

Based on your answers, you will likely be allowed in Canada. The final decision is taken at the border, by a border officer.

You must have spent the last 14 days (from the day you arrive in Canada) in either Saint-Pierre-et-Miquelon, or the United States.

You must:

  • have a quarantine plan
  • follow a mandatory quarantine period of 14 days
  • comply with any additional provincial or territorial requirements
