Find out if you can travel to Canada - Foreign national without symptoms - French

Effective date: 2020-06-29

You indicated that you are:

  • a foreign national
  • not showing signs and symptoms consistent with COVID-19
  • a French citizen who lives in Saint-Pierre and Miquelon

You must meet the entry requirements under the Immigration and Refugee Protection Act and provide appropriate travel and immigration documentation. Final determination regarding entry is made by a government representative at the port of entry based on the information presented to them at the time of entry into Canada. Failure to comply with the COVID-19 Emergency order is an offence under the Quarantine Act and could lead to imprisonment and/or fines.

Based on your answers, you will likely be allowed into Canada.

What you need to do next

  • have spent the last 14 days (from the day you arrive in Canada) in either Saint-Pierre-et-Miquelon, or the United States
  • read the mandatory quarantine requirements, you must follow a mandatory quarantine of 14 days when you reach your final destination
  • make sure you have a suitable place and plan to quarantine
  • read the provincial or territorial requirements
