Who can travel to Canada - Foreign national without symptoms - Essential service

Effective date: 2020-06-29

You indicated that you are:

  • a Canadian citizen, a permanent resident, a person registered under the Indian Act or a protected person

  • a person who will be moving of essential goods or people while in Canada (air crew, train crew, marine crew, truck driver)

Based on your answers, you can enter Canada.

Moving essential goods or people is generally exempt from quarantine for situations described under essential work. Final determination on quarantine exemption is made by a border services officer at the port of entry, based on the information presented to them at the time of entry into Canada.

If a border officer determines you are not quarantine exempt

You must:

  • provide details of how you intend to plan for quarantine
  • follow a mandatory quarantine period of 14 days

You may have to comply with additional provincial or territorial requirements.

Failure to comply with this order is an offence under the Quarantine Act and could lead to imprisonment and/or fines.
