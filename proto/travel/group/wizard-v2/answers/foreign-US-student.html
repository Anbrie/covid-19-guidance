Find out if you can travel to Canada - Foreign national without symptoms - Student - US

Effective date: 2020-06-29

You indicated that you are:

  • a foreign national
  • not showing signs and symptoms consistent with COVID-19
  • coming directly from the United States
  • an international student

You must meet the entry requirements under the Immigration and Refugee Protection Act and provide appropriate travel and immigration documentation. Final determination regarding entry is made by a government representative at the port of entry based on the information presented to them at the time of entry into Canada. Failure to comply with the COVID-19 Emergency order is an offence under the Quarantine Act and could lead to imprisonment and/or fines.

Based on your answers, you must prove it is essential that you need to enter Canada for your studies. If you have compelling proof, you will likely be allowed into Canada.

Acceptable reasons to enter Canada for your studies:

  • you’re already living in Canada and are returning
  • you need to be in Canada for your program (for laboratories, workshops)
  • your school does not have an online option
  • your school does have an online option, but it is not possible from your home country (due to internet restrictions, bandwidth limitation, or timezone differences where you would be awake at unreasonable hours)

What you need to do next

  • read the mandatory quarantine requirements: you must follow a mandatory quarantine of 14 days when you reach your final destination
  • make sure you have a suitable place to quarantine
  • read the provincial or territorial requirements
United States residents who attend a cross-border school in Canada

Cross-border school attendees are now subject to the mandatory 14 day quarantine requirement each time they seek entry into Canada and are not allowed to cross into Canada on a day-to-day basis. Need additional content about quarantine in residence, use of public transit, etc.

You must:

  • follow a mandatory quarantine period of 14 days when you reach your final destination
  • have a suitable place to quarantine
Students in a health field: quarantine exemptions

If you are working in Canada as a student in a health field, you will likely be exempt from quarantine as long as you do not directly care for persons 65 years of age or older within the 14-day period that begins on the day you enter Canada.

You must still comply with any additional provincial or territorial requirements.
