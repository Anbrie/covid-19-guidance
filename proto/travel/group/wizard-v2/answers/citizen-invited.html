Find out if you can travel to Canada - Citizen - National interest or invited

Effective date: 2020-06-29

You indicated that you are:

  • a Canadian citizen, a permanent resident, a person registered under the Indian Act, or a protected person
  • not showing signs and symptoms consistent with COVID-19
  • a person of national interest or invited to Canada to assist with COVID-19

Final determination regarding entry is made by a government representative at the port of entry based on the information presented to them at the time of entry into Canada. Failure to comply with the COVID-19 Emergency order is an offence under the Quarantine Act and could lead to imprisonment and/or fines.

Based on your answers, you can enter Canada.

You should be exempt from quarantine as long as you comply with any conditions imposed by the inviting Minister.

If a government representative at the border determines you are not quarantine exempt

You must:

  • read the mandatory quarantine requirements, you must follow a mandatory quarantine of 14 days when you reach your final destination
  • make sure you have a suitable place and plan to quarantine

You may have to comply with additional provincial or territorial requirements.
