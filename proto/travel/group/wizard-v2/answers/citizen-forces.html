Based on your answers, you can enter Canada.

Forces members and visiting forces are generally exempt from quarantine for situations described under essential work. If a border officer determines you are not exempt you must have a quarantine plan and follow a mandatory quarantine period of 14 days.

Their immediate family members must generally:

  • have a quarantine plan
  • follow a mandatory quarantine period of 14 days

It's possible an immediate family member could be exempt from quarantine if they are entering for an essential reason or have recieved written exemption.

Both forces members, visiting forces and their immediate family may have to comply with additional provincial or territorial requirements.

Failure to comply with this order is an offence under the Quarantine Act and could lead to imprisonment and/or fines.
