Déterminez si vous pouvez vous rendre au Canada - Ressortissants étrangers sans symptômes - non-U.S. entry

Date d'entrée en vigueur : 2020-06-29

Vous avez indiqué que :

  • vous êtes un ressortissant étranger
  • vous ne présentez pas de signes et de symptômes compatibles avec la COVID-19
  • vous ne venez pas directement des États-Unis
  • entering for essential work activities or employment reasons

Which best describes you or the nature of your work in Canada?

  • providing medical services or medical delivery
  • movement of essential goods or people (air crew, marine crew)
  • temporary foreign worker (farming, household, gasoline stations, food service, etc.)
  • commercial or research fishing-related activities
  • technician or specialist (supporting critical infrastructure)
  • commercial or research fishing-related activities
  • member of the Canadian Forces, visiting forces or their immediate family members
  • a person of national interest or invited to Canada
  • accredited diplomat or their immediate family members
  • another essential service
