Canada Recovery Caregiving Benefit (CRCB)

The Canada Recovery Caregiving Benefit (CRCB) gives income support to employed and self-employed Canadians who are unable to work because they have to care for their child under 12 years old or other dependent family members whose school, regular program or facility is closed due to COVID-19. The CRCB is administered by the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA).

If you are eligible for the CRCB, each household can receive $500 ($450 after taxes withheld) for each 1-week period.

If your situation continues past 1 week, you will need to apply again. You may re-apply up to a maximum of 26 weeks between September 27, 2020 and September 25, 2021.
