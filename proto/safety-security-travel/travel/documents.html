Travel insurance

If you are currently travelling outside Canada, we recommend that you consider returning to Canada as soon as possible. If you plan to travel outside Canada, travel insurance providers will no longer provide coverage for travel booked on or after March 13, 2020 when the official global travel advisory came into effect. This global advisory overrides other risk levels for all countries, including the United States, with the exception of areas for which we advise to avoid all travel (including regional advisories).


If you are already outside Canada, including the United States, choosing to stay abroad could change or complicate your travel health insurance coverage, including losing coverage for COVID-19 and other conditions. Your travel insurance provider may only give you a 10-day notice before placing limits on your insurance policy. It is your responsibility to know and understand the terms of your insurance policy. Read the fine print carefully and ask for help, if you need it. Check with your travel insurance provider to know more about your policy related to COVID-19.

Passports

Passport services in Canada

You can’t get passport services at Service Canada offices, including passport services offices, until further notice.

If you already submitted an application, we’ll process it when service returns to normal.

If you need to travel urgently

You can get passport services in Canada if you have a valid reason to travel urgently.

Urgent travel reasons include if you’re travelling

  • for a medical reason because you have a serious illness
  • because of the serious illness or death of someone you know
  • because you’ll have financial problems from the loss of a job or business
  • to support an essential service
  • for humanitarian reasons
    • You need a letter from your organization that lists the humanitarian reasons.

The loss of airfare or other travel expenses is not a reason for urgent travel.

To apply urgently, complete the online form to get a call back, or call the Service Canada Passport Call Centre directly at 1-800-567-6868.

Don’t apply if

  • you don’t meet the above criteria for urgent travel
  • you’re having symptoms such as coughing, difficulty breathing or a fever
  • you’re in isolation

If you don’t need your passport urgently, wait until services return to normal.

Passport services in the U.S.

If you need a passport for travel or to meet U.S. residency requirements, apply by mail or courier.

If you need a passport urgently, contact the nearest Government of Canada office abroad.

If you don’t need your passport urgently, wait until services return to normal.

Passport services abroad

We’re still accepting passport applications at many offices abroad if you need to

  • travel
  • meet residency requirements

Before you apply, contact the nearest Government of Canada office by phone or email before you go in person.

If you don’t need your passport urgently, wait until services return to normal.

Certificate of identity or refugee travel document

You can’t apply for a travel document at the Certificate of Identity Office until further notice. If you’ve already applied, we’ll process your application when service returns to normal.

If you need to travel urgently

You can get travel document services in Canada if you have a valid reason to travel urgently.

Urgent travel reasons include if you’re travelling

  • for a medical reason because you have a serious illness
  • because of the serious illness or death of someone you know
  • because you’ll have financial problems from the loss of a job or business
  • to support an essential service
  • for humanitarian reasons
    • You need a letter from your organization that lists the humanitarian reasons.

To apply urgently, fill out the Web form and

  • select the category for a certificate of identity or a refugee travel document
  • include your travel date
  • include the reason you need urgent services

Don’t apply if

  • you don’t meet the above criteria for urgent travel
  • you’re having symptoms such as coughing, difficulty breathing or a fever
  • you’re in isolation

If you don’t need your travel document urgently, wait until services return to normal.
