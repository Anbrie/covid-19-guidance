Canada’s COVID-19 Economic Response Plan

The Government of Canada is taking immediate, significant and decisive action to support Canadians and businesses facing hardship as a result of the global COVID-19 outbreak.

Services and information

For individuals

Individuals and families, people facing loss of income, Indigenous peoples, seniors, students and people who need it the most

For businesses

Avoiding layoffs and rehiring employees, access to credit, deferred payments, self-employed individuals, Indigenous businesses, supporting financial stability

Support for industries

Agriculture, agri-food, aquaculture and fisheries, culture, heritage and sports, air transportation, tourism, energy
