How much you can get for each eligibility period

The CESB provides a relief benefit for eligible students for a maximum of 16 weeks. The benefit provides a payment of $1,250 for each 4-week period from May to August 2020. If eligible, you may be able to get $2,000 for each 4-week period, if you meet at least one of the following requirements:

  • you are receiving the Disability Tax Credit (DTC) or other disability financial support from provincial or federal programs
  • you have children under the age of 18 or other dependants

How many eligibility periods you can apply for

The CESB is available for students who are looking for work, but cannot find work because of COVID-19.

Post-secondary students

Eligible post-secondary students, post-secondary graduates, and students who have recently left their post-graduate studies can apply for all 4 eligibility periods of the CESB, starting from the May 10 to June 6 eligibility period.

Graduating high school students

The CESB is only available to high school students who have graduated.

Your graduation date determines how many eligible periods you can apply for
