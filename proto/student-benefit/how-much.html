How much you can get

The CESB is a taxable benefit that provides relief for eligible students for a maximum of 16 weeks.

Payment amount

The benefit provides a payment of $1,250 for each 4-week period from May to August 2020. You may be able to get an extra $750 for each 4-week period, if you meet at least one of the following requirements:

  • you are receiving the Disability Tax Credit (DTC) or other disability financial support from provincial or federal programs
  • you have children under the age of 18 or other dependants

Eligibility periods

The number of eligibility periods you can apply for and the total amount you can receive, depends on whether you're a post-secondary or a graduating high school student.
