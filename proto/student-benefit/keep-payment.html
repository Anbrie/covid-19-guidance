Re-apply for the CESB

If your situation continues, you can re-apply for another 4-week eligible period.

Determine when to re-apply

When and how to expect your payment

If you are eligible for the CESB, you can expect $1,250 for the 4-week eligibility period you appled for, or $2,000 for those who have dependants or a disability.

The CESB is paid by direct deposit in 3 business days if you have direct deposit with the CRA. If you don’t, you should get a cheque in about 10 business days.
