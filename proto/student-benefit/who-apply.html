Who can apply

Application deadline

Applications for this benefit must be submitted before September 30, 2020.

To be eligible for the CESB, you must meet the following conditions:

    • you cannot find part-time or full-time work due to COVID-19
    • you are earning less than $1,000 (before taxes) from employment and self-employment income during the 4-week period you are applying for

We verify eligibility

The CRA will verify that you are eligible to receive the CESB. If you apply but are not eligible, we will contact you to make arrangements to repay any amounts you may owe.
