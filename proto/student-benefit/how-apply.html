Determine your eligibility periods

Each eligibility period is a 4-week period with a specific start and end date. When you apply, you will receive a payment for the specific eligibility period you applied for.

Post-secondary students and recent post-secondary graduates

You may apply for support for up to 16 weeks, starting from the May 10 to June 6 eligibility period.

or

Graduating high school students

You may apply for support starting with the eligibility period following your graduation. For example, if you graduate in June 2020, July 5 to August 1 is your first eligibility period.

Application deadline

All applications for this benefit must be submitted befor September 30, 2020.
