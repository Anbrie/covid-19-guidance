Determine your eligibility periods

Each eligibility period is a 4-week period with a specific start and end date. When you apply, you will receive a payment for the specific eligibility period you applied for.

Post-secondary students and recent post-secondary graduates

May apply for support for up to 16 weeks, starting from the May 10 to June 6 eligibility period.

or

Graduating high school students

May apply for support for up to 8 weeks, starting from the July 5 to August 1 eligibility period.

CESB is a taxable benefit

You will need to report any payments you received on next year's income tax return. An information slip will be made available for the 2020 tax year in CRA My Account under Tax Information Slips (T4 and more).
