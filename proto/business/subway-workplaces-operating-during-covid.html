Guidance for workplaces operating during COVID-19

Avoid risks to employees, customers and clients during the coronavirus disease (COVID-19) pandemic.

This is not applicable to workplaces where health care is provided.

First responders and health care workers should follow the occupational health and safety and infection prevention and control practices within their workplace.
