Canada Emergency Response Benefit (CERB)

The Canada Emergency Response Benefit (CERB) supports Canadians who have lost their income because of COVID-19. The sections below will help you understand the CERB and how to get the maximum benefit you are eligible for.

The CERB is a new benefit that you can apply for as early as April 6.

It supports individuals who may or may not have become unemployed, but who:

  • have lost their income because there is no longer work for them
  • can’t work because they are sick
  • have to stay home to take care of their children.

The CERB is not Employment Insurance (EI). It is a separate benefit that you may get on its own, or in addition to EI. If you are already on EI, you will get the CERB automatically.
