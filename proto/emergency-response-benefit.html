Canada Emergency Response Benefit

The Canada Emergency Response Benefit (CERB) supports Canadians who have lost their income because of COVID-19. This benefit supports those employees, contract workers and self-employed individuals that generally are not eligible for EI, and aren’t earning income because there is no longer demand for their services, or because they can’t work because they are sick, or have to stay home to take care of their children. The Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) administers this benefit.
