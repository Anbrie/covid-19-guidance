Canada Recovery Benefit (CRB)

The Canada Recovery Benefit (CRB) gives income support to employed and self-employed individuals whose income is directly affected by COVID-19. The benefit will assist people who are ready to work, and looking for work, but do not qualify for the new Employment Insurance (EI) benefits.

The CRB will provide $800 (before taxes withheld) per 2-week period, for up to 26 weeks.

The benefit is pending legislation. Information about eligibility and how to apply will be available after legislation has passed in the House of Commons and Senate.

    Review highlights of EI changes
    • You will now only need a minimum of 120 insurable hours (in which you paid into EI) for EI regular benefits
    • You may get a minimum rate of $400 per week for up to 26 weeks
    • You can still work, but your benefit will reduce by $0.50 for every dollar you earn (up to 90% of previous weekly earning)
    • If you qualify for EI, you may apply for it as of September 27, 2020
