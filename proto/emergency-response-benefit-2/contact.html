By phone:

Wait times

Given the current environment, we have fewer agents available to answer calls. You may experience longer than usual wait times to speak to an agent. We ask for your patience and understanding.

Reasons to call

At this time, we ask that Canadians only call if your questions are related to the filing of a 2019 income tax and benefit return, your benefits, or if you need help in setting up your My Account. For all other enquiries, we ask that Canadians consider calling back at a later time.

Before you call:

To verify your identity, you'll need your:

  • social insurance number (SIN)
  • mailing address
